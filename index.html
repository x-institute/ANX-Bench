02 / WHY

The missing half

Why this benchmark is worth building.

Thousands of tests ask what models can do. ANX-Bench asks how those capabilities register in the lives of the people beneath them—and makes the answer auditable.

01

Important

Decision-grade infrastructure for policy, responsible deployment, and labor research—not another transient headline percentage.

02

Innovative

Versioned human-response measurement joined to domain-specific capability, deployment, exposure, and locked event signals.

03

Insightful

Proximity effects, stated-versus-revealed gaps, and cross-cultural differences remain informative even when results are null.

04 / THE INSTRUMENT

Five measurement layers

No single method gets to be the truth.

The population panel anchors the benchmark. Four additional methods test, explain, and nowcast its signals without pretending their limitations disappear.

  1. 01

    Panel survey

    Seven domains, probability-based samples, and invariance-linked multi-item scales.

    RoleCalibrated reference series
  2. 02

    Behavioral tasks

    Costly choices involving money, effort, privacy, and human-over-AI review.

    TestsStated vs. revealed response
  3. 03

    Psychophysiology

    GSR, HRV, and pupillometry during standardized capability demonstrations.

    LimitArousal indicator, not anxiety proof
  4. 04

    Proximity experiments

    Randomized proximity, controllability, oversight, and capability information.

    IdentifiesMechanism through randomization
  5. 05

    Text nowcast

    A domain-labeled public-text signal calibrated against future panel waves.

    LimitExploratory between-wave signal

The scientific refusal

If the constructs do not link, we do not publish the index.

Refusing a false headline is a successful scientific result. Domain- and country-level series still ship where the evidence supports them.
